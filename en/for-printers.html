From the beginning, SCR Recycling has specialised in providing comprehensive services to printers.

Our offer

SCR analyses needs of our customers and proposes adequate and innovative solutions, such as:

     ● optimisation of internal logistics

     ● installation of appropriate equipment (presses, containers, tanks, integrated systems, ….)

     ● collections carried out regularly or on request

     ● comprehensive service (waste paper, plastics, offset sheet, hazardous waste…)

Why us?

Using our experience and expertise, you gain the opportunity to:

     ● reduce hidden costs of your business (internal transport, downtime…)

     ● increase revenues from the recycling of secondary raw materials,

     ● cooperate with a trustworthy partner (stability, integrity and professionalism guaranteed).

Companies and offices

SCR also provides services to companies and offices, advising in the management of waste.
